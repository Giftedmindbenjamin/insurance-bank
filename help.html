Frequently Asked Questions

10 articles

How do I reset my online banking password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll need to verify your identity using your registered email or phone number. Follow the instructions sent to your device to create a new password.

How can I set up automatic bill payments?

To set up automatic bill payments, go to the Bill Pay section, select the payee you want to set up recurring payments for, and choose "Schedule Automatic Payment". You can select the frequency, amount, and payment date.

What should I do if I see an unauthorized transaction?

If you notice an unauthorized transaction, contact our fraud department immediately at 1-800-123-4567. You can also dispute the transaction through your online banking dashboard. We'll investigate and help protect your account.

How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information in the Settings section under "Profile". There you can change your phone number, email address, and mailing address. Some changes may require verification for security purposes.
