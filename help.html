Frequently asked questions

10 articles

How do I reset my online banking password?

Click "Forgot password" on the login page. Verify your identity using your registered email or phone. Follow the instructions sent to your device to create a new password.

How can I set up automatic bill payments?

Go to Bill Pay, select the payee, and choose "Schedule automatic payment". You can set frequency, amount, and payment date.

What should I do if I see an unauthorized transaction?

Contact our fraud team immediately at +44 20 7946 0138. You can also dispute the transaction through your dashboard. We'll investigate promptly.

How do I update my contact information?

Go to Settings → Profile. There you can change your phone, email, or address. Some changes require verification.
